What They Are

What Are Chemical Peels?

Chemical peels are skin renewal treatments that use a provider selected peel solution to support exfoliation and refreshed looking skin.

Peel selection depends on skin type, concerns, sensitivity, history, downtime tolerance, and provider evaluation.

Safety

Provider Review and Safety Come First

Skin treatments should be selected with proper provider evaluation, realistic expectations, and careful aftercare planning.

Tell your provider if you are pregnant, breastfeeding, have active infection, open wounds, recent procedures, poor healing, implanted devices, skin conditions, medication concerns, recent sun exposure, allergies, or health issues that should be reviewed.

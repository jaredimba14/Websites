What It Is

What Is Hair Restoration?

Hair restoration at Elite VitaMed is a regenerative treatment approach for clients noticing thinning hair, shedding, reduced density, or early hair changes.

Depending on goals and candidacy, regenerative options such as PRP or PRF may be discussed as part of a hair restoration plan.

Hair thinning can be influenced by genetics, hormones, stress, nutrition, medications, medical conditions, postpartum changes, aging, and scalp health. Your provider will review your history and may recommend additional evaluation when appropriate.

Safety

Provider Review and Medical Screening Matter

Hair loss has many possible causes. Your provider reviews your history, medications, scalp health, and timeline before recommending treatment.

Tell your provider if you are pregnant, breastfeeding, have active infection, scalp disease, blood related concerns, autoimmune conditions, medication concerns, recent surgery, or health issues that should be reviewed.
